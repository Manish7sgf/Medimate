Privacy Policy

About MediMate Pro

MediMate Pro is an AI-powered healthcare assistant designed to provide preliminary symptom analysis and health guidance. Our system uses advanced machine learning algorithms trained on medical data to help users understand their symptoms better.

Data Collection & Usage

We collect and process the following information:

  • Symptom descriptions you provide
  • Medical history you share during consultations
  • User account information (username, email)
  • Diagnosis history for your personal records

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Providing accurate symptom analysis
  • Maintaining your health history
  • Improving our AI model accuracy
  • Generating personalized health insights

Data Security

Your privacy and security are our top priorities:

  • All data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Secure JWT-based authentication
  • No sharing of personal health information with third parties
  • Regular security audits and updates

Your Rights

  • Access your complete medical history anytime
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your health records
  • Opt-out of data analysis for model improvement

Important Disclaimers

  • Not a Substitute for Medical Care: MediMate Pro provides educational information only and does not replace professional medical advice
  • Emergency Situations: For severe or life-threatening symptoms, always call emergency services immediately
  • AI Limitations: Our AI model may not detect all conditions. Always consult a licensed healthcare provider for diagnosis
  • Accuracy: While we strive for accuracy, predictions are based on machine learning and should be verified by medical professionals
